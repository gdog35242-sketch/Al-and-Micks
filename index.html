Our Story

The Kind of BBQ
You Remember

slow-smoked, never rushed

Al & Mick's Barbeque has been a fixture on US-278 in Snead, Alabama — a place where the smoke never really stops rising and the portions are never too small. We're the kind of spot people drive out of their way for and tell their neighbors about.

Our pulled pork is tender and juicy, our baked beans are something people genuinely rave about, and every plate comes with two sides worth every bite. From the drive-through or seated inside, Al & Mick's is real Southern BBQ done right.
